Chris Davie Data Scientist - I solve business problems with data.

I'm a data science freelancer and consultant, and I enjoy using data to solve business problems and help businesses make better decisions; I typically gather and clean data, analyse it and create visualisations of the results. I have 4 years Python experience and have consulting experience, providing data analysis and visualisations. I have experience of algorithm development from my Physics background, and I am currently improving my machine learning and d3 skills.

I have a PhD in Computational Physics from Imperial College London, have published academic papers and given presentations at international conferences.
